Signature Tower

Naya Nazimabad, Karachi

CategoryResidential High Rise
ScopeArchitectural Design
Size190,000 SQ. FT.
StatusUnder Construction, 2021 Onward

Signature Tower is the largest scale project in the studio's portfolio, a residential high rise in Naya Nazimabad designed to bring a more considered architectural language to a fast growing part of Karachi. At close to 190,000 square feet, the project sits closer to the institutional work handled during my time at Habib Fida Ali Architects than a typical boutique commission.

The facade is organized around a rhythm of recessed balconies and vertical fins that break up the mass of the building while giving every unit usable outdoor space, a detail that gets lost in a lot of high density housing. Construction is ongoing, and the project remains one of the most technically demanding in the studio's current pipeline.
