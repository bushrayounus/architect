Coastal Retreat — Architecture

Hawksbay Beach, Karachi

CategoryResidential
ScopeArchitectural Design
Size16,500 SQ. FT.
StatusCompleted

Set on a quiet stretch of Hawksbay, this residence was designed to feel like an extension of the coastline itself. The brief called for a home that could withstand the exposure of a beachfront site while still feeling open and unguarded, so the massing was pulled apart into a series of low, layered volumes rather than a single dominant block.

Deep overhangs and recessed terraces shield the interiors from direct sun while framing long views of the water. Materials were chosen for how they age against salt air, textured stone, timber screening and a restrained palette that lets the light do most of the work through the day.

The result is a house that reads as calm from every angle, structured enough to hold up against a demanding site, and quiet enough to let the sea stay the main event.
