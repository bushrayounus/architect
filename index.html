About Studio BY

Formed by years of major corporate leadership; delivering highly personalized architectural and interior excellence globally.

am an architect driven by the belief that space should be purposeful, enduring, and meticulously crafted. My design philosophy is rooted in a deep understanding of contextual architecture, structural integrity, and the timeless interplay of materials, light, and form. Before establishing my independent practice, I spent years shaping major architectural landscapes as the Lead Architect at Habib Fida Ali Architects (HFA). Working directly alongside the principal leadership, I spearheaded some of the firm’s most complex and prestigious commissions—ranging from high-end private residences and corporate headquarters to high-rise developments and expansive educational campuses. Throughout my tenure at HFA, I didn't just design; I managed the critical lifecycle of architecture. My role involved leading dedicated design teams, translating complex client visions into buildable realities, coordinating intricate engineering systems, and overseeing strict on-site execution. This rigorous corporate and institutional foundation instilled in me an uncompromising standard for detail, execution, and project management. Today, my independent practice carries forward that rich legacy of architectural excellence, reimagined through a highly personalized, agile approach. We bring the heavy-weight technical expertise required for large-scale institutional projects into a boutique studio setting. Whether designing an intimate residential retreat or a commercial landmark, our commitment remains the same: creating spaces that are contextually relevant, structurally honest, and beautifully executed.
