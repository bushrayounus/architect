Luxury Suite — Sind Club

Karachi

CategoryHospitality
ScopeInterior Design
Size500 SQ. FT.
StatusProposal

This proposal reimagines a member suite at the Sind Club, one of Karachi's oldest institutions, without losing the sense of history the building carries. The challenge with a space this small was making every square foot earn its place while still feeling generous.

The scheme leans on warm wood paneling, brass detailing and a muted palette that nods to the club's colonial architecture, reworked with cleaner lines and better proportions. Custom joinery does double duty throughout, storage, seating and display folded into a single continuous gesture. Every material choice was made with longevity in mind, this is a space meant to be used daily by members for decades, not styled once for a photograph.
