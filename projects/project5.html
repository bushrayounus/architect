Restaurant

DHA Phase VIII, Karachi

CategoryHospitality
ScopeArchitecture & Interior
Size2,000 SQ. FT.
StatusUnder Construction

This restaurant in DHA Phase VIII was designed from the ground up, covering both the shell and the interior fit out. Working across both scopes at once meant structural decisions, like ceiling heights and column placement, could be made with the final dining experience in mind from day one rather than worked around later.

The layout is built around a mix of intimate seating and a more open communal area, with material choices, warm timber, matte plaster and brass accents, meant to hold up to daily restaurant use without losing character over time. Construction is currently underway.
