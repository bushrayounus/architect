Coastal Retreat — Interior

Hawksbay Beach, Karachi

CategoryResidential
ScopeInterior Design
Companion ToCoastal Retreat, Architecture
StatusCompleted

The interiors were developed alongside the architecture of the Hawksbay residence, so the two were never treated as separate exercises. Furniture layouts, ceiling lines and material transitions were worked out at the same time as the walls that hold them.

Inside, the palette softens without losing discipline, natural linen, honed stone and timber that carries the same tone as the exterior screening. Rooms are arranged to keep sightlines open to the sea, with furniture kept low so nothing competes with the view. Lighting was one of the more considered pieces of the brief, most fixtures sit low and warm for the evening, while the daytime experience relies almost entirely on the natural light the architecture was designed to capture.
