A corporate interior fit out designed around one main idea, that a workplace should feel calm rather than corporate in the sterile sense. The palette stays neutral and material driven, with warmth introduced through timber joinery and considered lighting rather than color.

Layout decisions were made around how the team actually moves through the space day to day, open work areas paired with quieter rooms for focused work and meetings, so the office supports the way people work rather than dictating it.
